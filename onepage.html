About Tanya

I am Tanya Alvarado, born and raised in Fresno, CA. I am a licensed, hairstylist with over 10 years of experience, and owner of Salon Posh & Posh Suites. When I was a little girl, I always loved doing hair on my Barbie's and sisters. I used to love watching the Olsen twins on their show, "Two Of A Kind". Their hairstyles were always so cute and stylish. As I got older, I knew that I wanted to be a hairstylist. Unfortunately, I took a different career path after High School. I tried the college experience at Fresno City College and HATED IT. I went into Dental Assisting and worked in the dental field for 3 years but hair still had my full interest. I eventually decided to go to beauty school. That has been the best decision I've ever made. Now, with over 10 years of experience as a licensed cosmetologist, I can say I still LOVE doing hair.

"The only way to do GREAT WORK is to LOVE what you do! "

Specializing

Color Corrections

A “color correction” is meant to fix a color service that had undesirable results. Most people can understand that a color correction is needed for brassy, orange highlights, or at-home color that came out differently than desired.

Sombre

Sombré is a soft, subtle ombre that typically blends higher up to achieve a more natural look. However, the ends remain all light, with only subtle hints of dimensional darkness going through.

Balayage

Balayage is a technique a stylist uses to highlight your hair, and can be used to achieve many different looks. With balayage, also known as hair painting, the lightener or hair color is painted on in such a way as to create a graduated, natural-looking effect from end to root and is all about creating beautiful blends of multi-dimensional color.
